Indiana Jones

Raiders of the Lost Ark

Directed by Steven Speilberg, Raiders of the Lost Ark portrays Harrison Ford as archaeoligst Indian Jones. The movie was a financial and critical succeess with many praising the the action of adventure of the film.

Temple of Doom

The sequel to Raiders of the Lost Ark, Temple of Doom was the second installment of the Indian Jones franchise. It was a prequel film with a darker tone and additional violence. It was still released to financial success but with mixed reviews.

The Last Crusade

The third installment of the Indian Jones franchise. After the mixed reviews of the previous installment, Speilberg used a lighter tone with the movie compared with Temple of Doom. The movie achieved a lot of success and additional critical praise for the inclusion of Sean Connery. His comedic chemistry with Ford was well-regarded.
